Getting here

Exact address details will be shared privately after confirmations. Hamont-Achel is close to the Dutch border and reachable from several airports and train routes.

Closest airports

Eindhoven is nearest; Brussels and Amsterdam Schiphol usually have strong international connections.

🚆

By train

From Brussels, head toward Neerpelt. From Amsterdam or Schiphol, train connections toward Weert work well.

Border-town calm, city chaos nearby.

Hamont-Achel is small, green and relaxed, surrounded by forests, beer culture and cycling routes. But bigger nights out are still within reach.

Eindhoven

Closest proper city, terraces, bars, student energy and Stratumseind nightlife.

Amsterdam

Canals, coffeeshops, crowds, nightlife and expensive but memorable chaos.

Maastricht

Old streets, terraces, good food and a calmer Burgundian vibe.

Brussels

International, messy, late-night food and proper club culture.

Antwerp

Stylish Belgian city culture, architecture, cafés and a dramatic train station.

Düsseldorf

Altstadt bars, cocktails, beer culture and a great Japanese food district.

Beer, food & touching grass.

Strong Trappist and abbey beer traditions, deceptively harmless tripels and dubbels, and the kind of nature that's perfect for recovering from too much Belgian beer and too little sleep.

🍺

Belgian beer

Achel and nearby abbey traditions — tripels, dubbels and dark beers that quietly ruin your evening.

🍟

Fries & stoofvlees

Belgian fries with rich Flemish beef stew — mandatory late-night and hangover food. Plus award-winning Achelse Blauwe cheese.

🌲

Nature & recovery

De Malpie heathland and the Leenderbos forests — quiet walking routes for slow, hungover afternoons.
