Getting here

03

Exact address shared privately after confirmations — but the region is surprisingly easy to reach from three countries.

By air

Three airports

Eindhoven is nearest; Brussels and Amsterdam Schiphol have the strongest international connections.

By rail

Easy trains

Toward Neerpelt from Brussels; toward Weert from Amsterdam or Schiphol. Better than you'd expect.

Beer, food & slow mornings

05
Drink

Trappist & abbey beer

Tripels, dubbels and dark beers that taste deceptively harmless while quietly ruining your evening. The Achel abbey tradition runs deep here.

Eat

Fries & stoofvlees

Belgian fries with rich Flemish beef stew — mandatory late-night and hangover food. Plus the award-winning Achelse Blauwe cheese.

Recover

Forests & heath

De Malpie heathland and the Leenderbos forests — quiet trails for touching grass after too much Discord and Belgian beer.
